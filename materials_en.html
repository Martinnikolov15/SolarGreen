Materials

Using solar energy requires recycling the solar panels.

We take back the materials they were built from and put them back into making new ones.

Glass +

The protective glass of a solar panel makes up about 70% of its weight. Using our advanced recycling technology, we recover high-purity glass suitable for container and, in the future, solar glass.
